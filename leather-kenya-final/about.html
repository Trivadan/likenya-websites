About us

  • Home
  • About us

With over 30 years experience, Leather Industries Kenya (LIK) produces high quality leather for the export market tailoring products to meet Customer's requirements.

Maintaining stringent quality control measures throughout the process, the entire production is done internally, from wet blue to crust to finished leather .

Careful selection of raw materials, good machinery and a dedicated workforce enable us to make high quality and value added leather products. Our tannery is one of the most technologically advanced in Sub-Saharan Africa.

LIK is a part of the Industrial Promotion Services (www.ipskenya.com) group of companies, which is an affiliate of the Aga Khan Development Network (AKDN)- www.akdn.org .
