ECONOMIC

In addition to creating employment at the tannery, the company sources skins and hides locally, thereby enhancing the livelihoods of many livestock farmers across the country.

ENVIRONMENT

The tannery has a fully fledged effluent treatment plant and has introduced a chrome recovery plant. This has significantly reduced costs and improved the effluent treatment process. In addition, the company to date has planted over 15,000 trees on its premises and the surrounding community.

SOCIAL

LIK implements a comprehensive workplace wellness programmes which aim to support employee's health and well being. The tanneries also have community outreach programmes within schools and the surrounding local community. Working with local government institutions and livestock farmers, the company also encourages the implementation of good flaying and fleshing practices to ensure quality hides and skins.
