Product Quality

Our leather

We source our leather from independent farmers, paying them a premium for better quality, ensuring that we get the best product and they get the best price. Maintaining rigorous quality control measures throughout the production process, from wet blue, crust, to finished leather is entirely undertaken in-house. Our leather is made from cowhides, goatskins, camel hides and sheepskins. Our finished leather is classified as either corrected grain or full grain.

Being one of the oldest finished leather producers in the region we have the advantage of experience when it comes to techniques and processes. Furthermore our recent overhaul and upgrade in machinery allows us to further improve quality and produce any type of leather our customers may require.

Environment and Community responsibility

As a project company of IPS we share a common ethos which can best be described as a desire to enhance the environments and quality of life of the communities we operate in. This is why we source as much of the raw materials that we can from the local community.

At LIK it is our belief that the environment shouldn't have to suffer for us to do business; which is partly why the tannery has a fully-fledged effluent treatment plant to ensure that no harmful materials make their way back into the habitat. LIK has also introduced a chrome recovery plant monitored by on site staff in conjunction with NEMA. This has significantly reduced costs and improved the effluent treatment process. In addition, the company to date has planted over 15,000 trees on its premises and the surrounding community.
